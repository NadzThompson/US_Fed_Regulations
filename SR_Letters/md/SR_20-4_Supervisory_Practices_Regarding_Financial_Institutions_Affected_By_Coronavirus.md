SUBJECT:
Supervisory Practices Regarding Financial Institutions Affected by Coronavirus
Applicability:
This letter is relevant to all institutions supervised by the Federal Reserve.
On March 9, the Federal Reserve Board and the other federal financial institution regulatory agencies and state bank regulators issued a statement to encourage financial institutions to meet the financial services needs of their customers and members in areas affected by the Coronavirus.
