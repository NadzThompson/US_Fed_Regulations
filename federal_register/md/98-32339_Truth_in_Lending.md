# Truth in Lending

**Proposed Rule** | 63 FR 67436 | Published 1998-12-07

**Document Number:** 98-32339

---

## Action

Proposed rule; official staff interpretation.

## Abstract

The Board is publishing for comment proposed revisions to the official staff commentary to Regulation Z (Truth in Lending). The commentary applies and interprets the requirements of Regulation Z. The proposed update addresses the prohibition against the issuance of unsolicited credit cards. It provides guidance on calculating payment schedules involving private mortgage insurance. In addition, the proposed update discusses credit sale transactions where downpayments include cash and property used as a trade-in.
