# Community Reinvestment Act

**Proposed Rule** | 85 FR 66410 | Published 2020-10-19

**Document Number:** 2020-21227

---

## Action

Advance notice of proposed rulemaking; request for comment.

## Abstract

The Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System (Board) is publishing for public comment an advance notice of proposed rulemaking (ANPR) to solicit public input regarding modernizing the Board's Community Reinvestment Act regulatory and supervisory framework. The Board is seeking comment on all aspects of the ANPR from all interested parties and also requests commenters to identify other issues that the Board should consider.
