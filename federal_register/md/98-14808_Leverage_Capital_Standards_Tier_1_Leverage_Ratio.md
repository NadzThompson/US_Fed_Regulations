# Leverage Capital Standards: Tier 1 Leverage Ratio

**Final Rule** | 63 FR 30369 | Published 1998-06-04

**Document Number:** 98-14808

---

## Action

Final rule.

## Abstract

The Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System (Board) is amending its Tier 1 leverage capital standard for bank holding companies. The effect of this final rule is to simplify the Board's leverage capital standard for bank holding companies and to incorporate the market risk capital rule into the leverage standard.
