# Consumer Leasing

**Final Rule** | 64 FR 16612 | Published 1999-04-06
**Effective:** 1999-03-31
**Document Number:** 99-8412

---

## Action

Final rule; official staff interpretation.

## Abstract

The Board is publishing revisions to the official staff commentary to Regulation M, which implements the Consumer Leasing Act. The commentary applies and interprets the requirements of the regulation. The update provides guidance on disclosures for lease renegotiations and extensions, official fees and taxes, multiple-item leases, and advertisements.
