# Equal Credit Opportunity

**Final Rule** | 68 FR 53491 | Published 2003-09-11
**Effective:** 2004-01-01
**Document Number:** 03-23175

---

## Action

Technical amendment.

## Abstract

The Board is publishing a technical amendment to Regulation B (Equal Credit Opportunity). The amendment updates the model application form "Uniform Residential Loan Application" (Freddie Mac 65/Fannie Mae 1003) in Appendix B of the regulation.
