# Proposed Agency Information Collection Activities; Comment Request

**Notice** | 88 FR 56627 | Published 2023-08-18

**Document Number:** 2023-17829

---

## Action

Notice, request for comment.

## Abstract

The Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System (Board) invites comment on a proposal to extend for three years, without revision, the Disclosure Requirements Associated with the Consumer Financial Protection Bureau's (CFPB) Regulation DD (FR DD; OMB No. 7100-0271).
