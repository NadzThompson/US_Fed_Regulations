# Loan Guarantees for Defense Production

**Final Rule** | 61 FR 52875 | Published 1996-10-09
**Effective:** 1996-10-09
**Document Number:** 96-25867

---

## Action

Final rule.

## Abstract

The Board is repealing its Regulation V on loan guarantees for defense production as obsolete. This action does not represent any policy change, but rather eliminates an outmoded regulation and reduces regulatory burden.
