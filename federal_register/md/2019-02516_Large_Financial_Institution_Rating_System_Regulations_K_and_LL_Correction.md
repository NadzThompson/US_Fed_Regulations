# Large Financial Institution Rating System; Regulations K and LL; Correction

**Final Rule** | 84 FR 4309 | Published 2019-02-15
**Effective:** 2019-02-15
**Document Number:** 2019-02516

---

## Action

Final rule; correcting amendments.

## Abstract

On November 21, 2018, the Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System (Board) published a final rule in the Federal Register regarding the Large Financial Institution Rating System. That document included two typographical errors in "Appendix A--Text of Large Financial Institution Rating System" relating to the description of the conditionally meets expectation rating. This document corrects those typographical errors.
