# Agency Information Collection Activities: Announcement of Board Approval Under Delegated Authority and Submission to OMB

**Notice** | 90 FR 14441 | Published 2025-04-02

**Document Number:** 2025-05645

---

## Action

On November 18, 2024, the Board published a notice in the Federal Register 89 FR 90287) requesting public comment for 60 days on the extension, without revision, of the FR 2082. The comment period for this notice expired on January 14, 2025. The Board did not receive any comments.

## Abstract

The Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System (Board) is adopting a proposal to extend for three years, without revision, the Registration of a Securities Holding Company (FR 2082; OMB No. 7100- 0347).
