# Federal Open Market Committee; Rules Regarding Availability of Information

**Final Rule** | 62 FR 61217 | Published 1997-11-17
**Effective:** 1997-12-17
**Document Number:** 97-29965

---

## Action

Final rule.

## Abstract

The Federal Open Market Committee (Committee) hereby amends its Rules Regarding Availability of Information (Rules) to reflect recent changes in the Freedom of Information Act (FOIA) as a result of the Electronic Freedom of Information Act Amendments (EFOIA).
