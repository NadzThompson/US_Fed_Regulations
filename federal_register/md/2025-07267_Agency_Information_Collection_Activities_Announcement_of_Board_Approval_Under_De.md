# Agency Information Collection Activities: Announcement of Board Approval Under Delegated Authority and Submission to OMB

**Notice** | 90 FR 17598 | Published 2025-04-28

**Document Number:** 2025-07267

---

## Action

On January 13, 2025, the Board published a notice in the Federal Register (90 FR 2700) requesting public comment for 60 days on the extension, without revision, of the FR II. The comment period for this notice expired on March 14, 2025. The Board received one comment. The comment was in support of the extension of the FII as proposed.

## Abstract

The Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System (Board) is adopting a proposal to extend for three years, without revision, the Recordkeeping and Disclosure Requirements Associated with Regulation II (FR II; OMB No. 7100-0349).
