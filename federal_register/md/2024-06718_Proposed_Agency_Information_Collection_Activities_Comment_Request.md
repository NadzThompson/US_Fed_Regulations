# Proposed Agency Information Collection Activities; Comment Request

**Notice** | 89 FR 22150 | Published 2024-03-29

**Document Number:** 2024-06718

---

## Action

Notice, request for comment.

## Abstract

The Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System (Board) invites comment on a proposal to extend for three years, with revision, the Reporting, Recordkeeping, and Disclosure Requirements Associated with Regulation YY (FR YY; OMB No. 7100-0350).
