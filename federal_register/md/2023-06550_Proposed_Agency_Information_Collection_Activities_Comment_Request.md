# Proposed Agency Information Collection Activities; Comment Request

**Notice** | 88 FR 19145 | Published 2023-03-30

**Document Number:** 2023-06550

---

## Action

Notice, request for comment.

## Abstract

The Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System (Board) invites comment on a proposal to extend for three years, with revision, the Ongoing Intermittent Survey of Households (FR 3016; OMB No. 7100- 0150).
