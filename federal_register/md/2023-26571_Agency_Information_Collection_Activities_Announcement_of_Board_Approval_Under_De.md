# Agency Information Collection Activities: Announcement of Board Approval Under Delegated Authority and Submission to OMB

**Notice** | 88 FR 84141 | Published 2023-12-04

**Document Number:** 2023-26571

---

## Action

On August 18, 2023, the Board published a notice in the Federal Register (88 FR 56622) requesting public comment for 60 days on the extension, without revision, of the Reports of Deposits collection. The comment period for this notice expired on October 17, 2023. The Board did not receive any comments.

## Abstract

The Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System (Board) is adopting a proposal to extend for three years, without revision, the Reports of Deposits (FR 2900, FR 2915; OMB No. 7100-0087).
