# Transactions Between Banks and Their Affiliates; Correction

**Proposed Rule** | 66 FR 33649 | Published 2001-06-25

**Document Number:** 01-15869

---

## Action

Notice of proposed rulemaking; correction.

## Abstract

This document supplements the preamble to a proposed Regulation W published in the Federal Register of May 11, 2001, regarding sections 23A and 23B of the Federal Reserve Act (66 FR 24186). This supplement seeks comment on whether the Board should exempt extensions of credit by a bank to any person that are secured by shares of a mutual fund for which the bank or an affiliate of the bank acts as investment adviser.
