# Truth in Lending

**Final Rule** | 72 FR 71058 | Published 2007-12-14
**Effective:** 2008-01-14
**Document Number:** E7-24222

---

## Action

Final rule; technical amendment.

## Abstract

The Board is revising the official staff commentary to Regulation Z, which implements the Truth in Lending Act, to clarify an amendment published on November 9, 2007. The clarification and the earlier amendment relate to the electronic delivery of disclosures under Regulation Z.
