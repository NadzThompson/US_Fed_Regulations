# Rules Regarding Equal Opportunity

**Final Rule** | 61 FR 251 | Published 1996-01-04
**Effective:** 1996-02-05
**Document Number:** 96-90

---

## Action

Final rule.

## Abstract

The Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System (the Board) has amended its Rules Regarding Equal Opportunity (Rules) to correct an ambiguity in the provision regarding access to the investigative file. The Rules set out the complaint processing procedures governing complaints by Board employees and applicants for employment alleging discrimination in employment, and related matters.
