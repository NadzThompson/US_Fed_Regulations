# Proposed Agency Information Collection Activities; Comment Request

**Notice** | 88 FR 46164 | Published 2023-07-19

**Document Number:** 2023-15245

---

## Action

Notice, request for comment.

## Abstract

The Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System (Board) invites comment on a proposal to extend for three years, without revision, the Recordkeeping Requirements Associated with Regulation H (Real Estate Lending Standards Regulation for State Member Banks) (FR H-5; OMB No. 7100-0261).
