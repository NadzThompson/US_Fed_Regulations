# Credit by Brokers and Dealers; List of Foreign Margin Stocks

**Final Rule** | 69 FR 10601 | Published 2004-03-08

**Document Number:** 04-5103

---

## Action

Final rule.

## Abstract

The List of Foreign Margin Stocks (List) is composed of certain foreign equity securities that qualify as margin securities under Regulation T. The Foreign List has been published twice a year by the Board since 1999. The Board is removing all 51 stocks from the current List because they have not been recertified as required under procedures approved by the Board in 1990. The Board will publish a new List if eligible securities are identified pursuant to these listing procedures.
