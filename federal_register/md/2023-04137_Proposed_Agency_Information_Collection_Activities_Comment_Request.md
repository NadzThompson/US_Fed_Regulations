# Proposed Agency Information Collection Activities; Comment Request

**Notice** | 88 FR 12936 | Published 2023-03-01

**Document Number:** 2023-04137

---

## Action

Notice, request for comment.

## Abstract

The Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System (Board) invites comment on a proposal to extend for three years, without revision, the Reporting and Recordkeeping Requirements Associated with Regulation KK (FR KK; OMB No. 7100-0364).
