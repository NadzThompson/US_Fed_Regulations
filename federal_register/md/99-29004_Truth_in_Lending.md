# Truth in Lending

**Proposed Rule** | 64 FR 60368 | Published 1999-11-05

**Document Number:** 99-29004

---

## Action

Proposed rule.

## Abstract

The Board is publishing for comment proposed revisions to the official staff commentary to Regulation Z (Truth in Lending). The commentary applies and interprets the requirements of Regulation Z. The proposed update addresses short-term cash advances commonly called ``payday loans'' and includes technical revisions.
