# Agency Information Collection Activities: Announcement of Board Approval Under Delegated Authority and Submission to OMB

**Notice** | 90 FR 17595 | Published 2025-04-28

**Document Number:** 2025-07265

---

## Action

On December 6, 2024, the Board published a notice in the Federal Register (89 FR 96979) requesting public comment for 60 days on the extension, without revision, of the FR 2231. The comment period for this notice expired on February 4, 2025. The Board did not receive any comments.

## Abstract

The Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System (Board) is adopting a proposal to extend for three years, without revision, the Computer-Security Incident Notification (FR 2231; OMB No. 7100-0384).
