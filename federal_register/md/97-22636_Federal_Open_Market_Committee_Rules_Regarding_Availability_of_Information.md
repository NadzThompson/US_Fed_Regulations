# Federal Open Market Committee; Rules Regarding Availability of Information

**Proposed Rule** | 62 FR 45178 | Published 1997-08-26

**Document Number:** 97-22636

---

## Action

Proposed rule.

## Abstract

The Federal Open Market Committee (Committee) hereby proposes to amend its Rules Regarding Availability of Information (Rules) to reflect recent changes in the Freedom of Information Act (FOIA) as a result of the Electronic Freedom of Information Act Amendments (EFOIA).
