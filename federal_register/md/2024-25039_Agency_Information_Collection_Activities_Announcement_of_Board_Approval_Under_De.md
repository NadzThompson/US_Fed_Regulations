# Agency Information Collection Activities: Announcement of Board Approval Under Delegated Authority and Submission to OMB

**Notice** | 89 FR 85972 | Published 2024-10-29

**Document Number:** 2024-25039

---

## Action

On June 7, 2024, the Board published a notice in the Federal Register (89 FR 48645) requesting public comment for 60 days on the extension, without revision, of the FR Z. The comment period for this notice expired on August 6, 2024. The Board did not receive any comments.

## Abstract

The Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System (Board) is adopting a proposal to extend for three years, without revision, the Recordkeeping and Disclosure Requirements Associated with CFPB's Regulation Z (FR Z; OMB No. 7100-0199).
