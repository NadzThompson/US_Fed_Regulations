# International Banking Operations

**Proposed Rule** | 68 FR 32434 | Published 2003-05-30

**Document Number:** 03-13371

---

## Action

Notice of proposed rulemaking.

## Abstract

The Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System (Board) is seeking public comment on a proposal to require Edge and Agreement corporations and U.S. branches, agencies and other offices of foreign banks supervised by the Board to establish and maintain procedures reasonably designed to assure and monitor compliance with the Bank Secrecy Act and the regulations issued thereunder.
