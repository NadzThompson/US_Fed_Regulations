# Home Mortgage Disclosure

**Proposed Rule** | 68 FR 11010 | Published 2003-03-07

**Document Number:** 03-5365

---

## Action

Proposed rule; official staff commentary.

## Abstract

In 2002, the Board revised Regulation C and imposed new data collection requirements with an effective date of January 1, 2004. This proposal would revise the official staff commentary to Regulation C to provide transition rules for applications received before January 1, 2004, on which final action is taken on or after January 1, 2004.
