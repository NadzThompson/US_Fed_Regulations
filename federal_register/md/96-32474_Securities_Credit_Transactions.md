# Securities Credit Transactions

**Proposed Rule** | 61 FR 67503 | Published 1996-12-23

**Document Number:** 96-32474

---

## Action

Proposed rule; extension of comment period.

## Abstract

The Board is extending the comment period on its proposal to amend its margin regulations, Regulations G, T, and U, to give the public additional time to comment on the proposal. The Secretary of the Board, acting pursuant to delegated authority, has extended the comment period from December 26, 1996, to January 31, 1997, to give the public additional time to provide comments.
