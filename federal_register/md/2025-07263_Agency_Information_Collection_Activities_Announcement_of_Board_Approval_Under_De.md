# Agency Information Collection Activities: Announcement of Board Approval Under Delegated Authority and Submission to OMB

**Notice** | 90 FR 17596 | Published 2025-04-28

**Document Number:** 2025-07263

---

## Action

On December 6, 2024, the Board published a notice in the Federal Register (89 FR 96974) requesting public comment for 60 days on the extension, without revision, of the FR LL-10(e). The comment period for this notice expired on February 4, 2025. The Board did not receive any comments.

## Abstract

The Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System (Board) is adopting a proposal to extend for three years, without revision, the Application to Become a Savings and Loan Holding Company or to Acquire a Savings Association or Savings and Loan Holding Company (FR LL-10(e); OMB No. 7100-0336).
