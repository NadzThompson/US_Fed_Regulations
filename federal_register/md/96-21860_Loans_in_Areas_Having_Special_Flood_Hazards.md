# Loans in Areas Having Special Flood Hazards

**Final Rule** | 61 FR 45684 | Published 1996-08-29
**Effective:** 1996-10-04
**Document Number:** 96-21860

---

## Action

Joint final rule.

## Abstract

The Comptroller of the Currency (OCC), Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System (Board), Federal Deposit Insurance Corporation (FDIC), Office of Thrift Supervision (OTS), and National Credit Union Administration (NCUA) are amending their regulations, and the Farm Credit Administration (FCA) is issuing new regulations, regarding loans in areas having special flood hazards. This action is required by statute to implement the provisions of the National Flood Insurance Reform Act of 1994. This joint final rule establishes new escrow requirements for flood insurance premiums, adds references to the statutory authority and the requirement for lenders and servicers to ``force place'' flood insurance under certain circumstances, enhances flood hazard notice requirements, sets forth new authority for lenders to charge fees for determining whether a property is located in a special flood hazard area, and contains various other provisions necessary to implement the National Flood Insurance Reform Act of 1994.
