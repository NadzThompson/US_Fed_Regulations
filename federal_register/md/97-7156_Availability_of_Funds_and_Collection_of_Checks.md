# Availability of Funds and Collection of Checks

**Final Rule** | 62 FR 13801 | Published 1997-03-24
**Effective:** 1997-04-28
**Document Number:** 97-7156

---

## Action

Final rule.

## Abstract

The Board has adopted amendments to its Regulation CC relating to the availability of funds and collection of checks. The amendments do not represent any major policy changes and are intended to clarify the regulation and, in some cases, reduce the compliance burden for depository institutions.
