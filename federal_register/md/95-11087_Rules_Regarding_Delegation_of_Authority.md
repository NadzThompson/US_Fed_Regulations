# Rules Regarding Delegation of Authority

**Final Rule** | 60 FR 22256 | Published 1995-05-05
**Effective:** 1995-06-05
**Document Number:** 95-11087

---

## Action

Final rule.

## Abstract

The Board is amending its delegation rules to allow Federal Reserve Banks to approve certain public welfare investments by state member banks under the Board's Regulation H. This amendment should provide for more expeditious processing of these requests.
