# Proposed Agency Information Collection Activities; Comment Request

**Notice** | 89 FR 16567 | Published 2024-03-07

**Document Number:** 2024-04892

---

## Action

Notice, request for comment.

## Abstract

The Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System (Board) invites comment on a proposal to extend for three years, without revision, the Recordkeeping and Disclosure Requirements Associated with the CFPB's Regulation M (FR M; OMB No. 7100-0202).
