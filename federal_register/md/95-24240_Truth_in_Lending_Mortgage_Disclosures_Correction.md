# Truth in Lending; Mortgage Disclosures; Correction

**Final Rule** | 60 FR 50399 | Published 1995-09-29
**Effective:** 1995-09-25
**Document Number:** 95-24240

---

## Action

Technical Correction to final regulation.

## Abstract

This document contains a correction to the final rule (Docket No. R-0858) which was published Friday, March 24, 1995 (60 FR 15463). The amendments to Regulation Z concerned new disclosure requirements on reverse mortgage transactions (as well as on certain home loans bearing rates or fees above a certain percentage or amount).
