# Transactions With Affiliates

**Proposed Rule** | 60 FR 62050 | Published 1995-12-04

**Document Number:** 95-29425

---

## Action

Notice of proposed rulemaking.

## Abstract

The Board is proposing to conform the definition of capital stock and surplus for purposes of section 23A of the Federal Reserve Act to the definition of unimpaired capital and unimpaired surplus used in Regulation O and to the definition of capital and surplus used by Office of the Comptroller of the Currency (OCC) in calculating the limit on loans by a national bank to a single borrower. The proposed rule seeks to reduce the burden for member banks and other insured depository institutions monitoring lending to their affiliates.
