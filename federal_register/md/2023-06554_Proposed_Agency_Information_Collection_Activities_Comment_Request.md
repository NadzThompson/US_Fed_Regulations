# Proposed Agency Information Collection Activities; Comment Request

**Notice** | 88 FR 19146 | Published 2023-03-30

**Document Number:** 2023-06554

---

## Action

Notice, request for comment.

## Abstract

The Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System (Board) invites comment on a proposal to extend for three years, with revision, the Joint Statement for Assessing the Diversity Policies and Practices of Entities Regulated by the Agencies (FR 2100; OMB No. 7100-0368).
