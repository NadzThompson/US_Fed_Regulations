# Availability of Funds and Collection of Checks; Correction

**Final Rule** | 68 FR 37957 | Published 2003-06-26
**Effective:** 2003-07-28
**Document Number:** 03-16051

---

## Action

Final rule; correction.

## Abstract

The Board of Governors is correcting the supplementary information that it provided in connection with a final rule updating the routing numbers for Federal Reserve Banks and Federal Home Loan Banks, which was published in the Federal Register of May 28, 2003.
