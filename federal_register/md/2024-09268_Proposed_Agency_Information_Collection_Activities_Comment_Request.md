# Proposed Agency Information Collection Activities; Comment Request

**Notice** | 89 FR 34245 | Published 2024-04-30

**Document Number:** 2024-09268

---

## Action

Notice, request for comment.

## Abstract

The Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System (Board) invites comment on a proposal to extend for three years, with revision, the Application for a Foreign Organization to Acquire a U.S. Bank or Bank Holding Company (FR Y-3F; OMB No. 7100-0119).
