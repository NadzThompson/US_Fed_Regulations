# Equal Credit Opportunity; Electronic Fund Transfers; Consumer Leasing; Truth in Lending; Truth in Savings

**Proposed Rule** | 64 FR 69963 | Published 1999-12-15

**Document Number:** 99-32303

---

## Action

Request for comments; reopening and extension of comment period.

## Abstract

On September 14, 1999, the Board published for public comment revised proposals that would permit electronic delivery of federally mandated disclosures under five consumer protection regulations: B (Equal Credit Opportunity), E (Electronic Fund Transfers), M (Consumer Leasing), Z (Truth in Lending), and DD (Truth in Savings). The Board is reopening and extending the comment period in order to obtain additional comments from individual consumers, primarily by conducting focus group interviews.
