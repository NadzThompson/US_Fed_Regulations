# Equal Credit Opportunity

**Proposed Rule** | 62 FR 37166 | Published 1997-07-11

**Document Number:** 97-18097

---

## Action

Proposed rule.

## Abstract

The Board is proposing to amend certain model forms in its Regulation B on equal credit opportunity to reflect recent statutory amendments to the Fair Credit Reporting Act (FCRA) disclosures contained in those forms. Creditors have the option of including the FCRA disclosures with the notice of action taken required under Regulation B.
