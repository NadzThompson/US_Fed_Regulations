# Agency Information Collection Activities: Announcement of Board Approval Under Delegated Authority and Submission to OMB

**Notice** | 90 FR 2702 | Published 2025-01-13

**Document Number:** 2025-00482

---

## Action

On July 30, 2024, the Board published a notice in the Federal Register (89 FR 61116) requesting public comment for 60 days on the extension, without revision, of the FR 3066. The comment period for this notice expired on September 30, 2024. The Board received one comment.

## Abstract

The Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System (Board) is adopting a proposal to extend for three years, without revision, the Federal Reserve Payments Study (FR 3066; OMB No. 7100-0351).
