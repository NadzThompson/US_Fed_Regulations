# Proposed Agency Information Collection Activities; Comment Request

**Notice** | 88 FR 66843 | Published 2023-09-28

**Document Number:** 2023-21161

---

## Action

Notice, request for comment.

## Abstract

The Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System (Board) invites comment on a proposal to extend for three years, with revision, the Recordkeeping and Disclosure Requirements Associated with Regulation O (FR O; OMB No. 7100-0382).
