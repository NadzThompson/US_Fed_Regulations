# Proposed Agency Information Collection Activities; Comment Request

**Notice** | 89 FR 22147 | Published 2024-03-29

**Document Number:** 2024-06713

---

## Action

Notice, request for comment.

## Abstract

The Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System (Board) invites comment on a proposal to extend for three years, without revision, the Recordkeeping Guidance Associated with Changes in Foreign Investments Made Pursuant to Regulation K (FR 2064; OMB No. 7100-0109).
