# Proposed Agency Information Collection Activities; Comment Request

**Notice** | 89 FR 54463 | Published 2024-07-01

**Document Number:** 2024-14372

---

## Action

Notice, request for comment.

## Abstract

The Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System (Board) invites comment on a proposal to extend for three years, without revision, the Recordkeeping and Disclosure Requirements Associated with Consumer Financial Protection Bureau's (CFPB's) Regulation B (FR B; OMB No. 7100-0201).
