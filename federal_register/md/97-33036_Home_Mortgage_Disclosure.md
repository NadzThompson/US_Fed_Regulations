# Home Mortgage Disclosure

**Final Rule** | 62 FR 66259 | Published 1997-12-18
**Effective:** 1998-01-01
**Document Number:** 97-33036

---


## Abstract

The Board is publishing revisions to its staff commentary that interprets the requirements of Regulation C (Home Mortgage Disclosure). The Board is required to adjust annually the asset-size exemption threshold for depository institutions based on the annual percentage change in the Consumer Price Index for Urban Wage Earners and Clerical Workers. The adjustment reflects changes for the twelve-month period ending in November. In 1998, depository institutions with assets totaling $29 million or less are not required to collect data.
