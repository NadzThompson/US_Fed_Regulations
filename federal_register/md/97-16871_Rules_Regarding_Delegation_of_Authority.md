# Rules Regarding Delegation of Authority

**Final Rule** | 62 FR 34617 | Published 1997-06-27
**Effective:** 1997-10-01
**Document Number:** 97-16871

---

## Action

Final rule.

## Abstract

The Board is amending its delegation rules to allow the Secretary of the Board to determine the Federal Reserve District in which an institution is located for purposes of Federal Reserve membership and reserve account maintenance. This amendment should provide for more expeditious handling of location determinations.
