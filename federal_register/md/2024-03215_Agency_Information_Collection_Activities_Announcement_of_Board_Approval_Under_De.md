# Agency Information Collection Activities: Announcement of Board Approval Under Delegated Authority and Submission to OMB

**Notice** | 89 FR 12341 | Published 2024-02-16

**Document Number:** 2024-03215

---

## Action

On September 28, 2023, the Board published a notice in the Federal Register (88 FR 66847) requesting public comment for 60 days on the extension, without revision, of the FR 4035. The comment period for this notice expired on November 27, 2023. The Board did not receive any comments.

## Abstract

The Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System (Board) is adopting a proposal to extend for three years, without revision, the Reporting, Recordkeeping, and Disclosure Requirements Associated with Rules Regarding Availability of Information (FR 4035 collection identifier; OMB No. 7100-0381).
