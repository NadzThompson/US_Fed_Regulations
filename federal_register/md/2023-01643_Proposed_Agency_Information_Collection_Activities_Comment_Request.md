# Proposed Agency Information Collection Activities; Comment Request

**Notice** | 88 FR 5340 | Published 2023-01-27

**Document Number:** 2023-01643

---

## Action

Notice, request for comment.

## Abstract

The Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System (Board) invites comment on a proposal to extend for three years, without revision, the Filings Related to the Gramm-Leach-Bliley Act (FR 4010, FR 4011, FR 4012, FR 4017, FR 4019, FR 4023; OMB No. 7100-0292).
