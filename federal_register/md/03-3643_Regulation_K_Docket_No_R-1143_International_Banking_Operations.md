# Regulation K; Docket No. R-1143; International Banking Operations

**Final Rule** | 68 FR 7898 | Published 2003-02-19

**Document Number:** 03-3643

---

## Action

Interpretation.

## Abstract

The Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System has issued an interpretation concerning the underwriting by foreign banks of securities to be distributed in the United States. The interpretation clarifies that a foreign bank that wishes to engage in such activity must either be a financial holding company or have authority to engage in underwriting activity under section 4(c)(8) of the Bank Holding Company Act.
