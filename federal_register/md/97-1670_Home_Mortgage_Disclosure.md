# Home Mortgage Disclosure

**Final Rule** | 62 FR 3603 | Published 1997-01-24

**Document Number:** 97-1670

---

## Action

Interim rule.

## Abstract

The Board is publishing an interim rule that amends Regulation C (Home Mortgage Disclosure). The amendment increases the asset-size exemption threshold for depository institutions from $10 million to $28 million.
