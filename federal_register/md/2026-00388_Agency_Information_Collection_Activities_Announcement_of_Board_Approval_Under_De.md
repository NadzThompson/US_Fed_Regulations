# Agency Information Collection Activities: Announcement of Board Approval Under Delegated Authority and Submission to OMB

**Notice** | 91 FR 1185 | Published 2026-01-12

**Document Number:** 2026-00388

---

## Action

On July 16, 2025, the Board published a notice in the Federal Register (90 FR 32008) requesting public comment for 60 days on the extension, without revision, of the FR 1374. The comment period for this notice expired on September 15, 2025. The Board did not receive any comments.

## Abstract

The Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System (Board) is adopting a proposal to extend for three years, without revision, the Intermittent Survey of Businesses (FR 1374; OMB No. 7100-0302).
