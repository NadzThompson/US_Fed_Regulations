# Agency Information Collection Activities: Announcement of Board Approval Under Delegated Authority and Submission to OMB

**Notice** | 89 FR 60893 | Published 2024-07-29

**Document Number:** 2024-16597

---

## Action

On April 29, 2024, the Board published a notice in the Federal Register (89 FR 33348) requesting public comment for 60 days on the extension, without revision, of the FR K-1. The comment period for this notice expired on June 28, 2024. The Board did not receive any comments.

## Abstract

The Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System (Board) is adopting a proposal to extend for three years, without revision, the International Applications and Prior Notifications under Subparts A and C of Regulation K (FR K-1; OMB No. 7100-0107).
