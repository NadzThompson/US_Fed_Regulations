# Agency Information Collection Activities: Announcement of Board Approval Under Delegated Authority and Submission to OMB

**Notice** | 90 FR 9158 | Published 2025-02-07

**Document Number:** 2025-02363

---

## Action

On October 2, 2024, the Board published a notice in the Federal Register (89 FR 80241) requesting public comment for 60 days on the extension, without revision, of the FR Y-8. The comment period for this notice expired on December 2, 2024. The Board did not receive any comments.

## Abstract

The Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System (Board) is adopting a proposal to extend for three years, without revision, the Holding Company Report of Insured Depository Institutions' Section 23A Transactions with Affiliates (FR Y-8; OMB No. 7100-0126).
