# Proposed Agency Information Collection Activities; Comment Request

**Notice** | 88 FR 66847 | Published 2023-09-28

**Document Number:** 2023-21158

---

## Action

Notice, request for comment.

## Abstract

The Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System (Board) invites comment on a proposal to extend for three years, without revision, the Reporting, Recordkeeping, and Disclosure Requirements Associated with Rules Regarding Availability of Information (FR 4035; OMB No. 7100-0381).
