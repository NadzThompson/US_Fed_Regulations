# Agency Information Collection Activities: Announcement of Board Approval Under Delegated Authority and Submission to OMB

**Notice** | 90 FR 14441 | Published 2025-04-02

**Document Number:** 2025-05648

---

## Action

On October 29, 2024, the Board published a notice in the Federal Register (89 FR 85973) requesting public comment for 60 days on the extension, without revision, of the FR K. The comment period for this notice expired on December 30, 2024. The Board did not receive any comments.

## Abstract

The Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System (Board) is adopting a proposal to extend for three years, without revision, the Recordkeeping Requirements of Regulation H and Regulation K Associated with the Procedures for Monitoring Bank Secrecy Act Compliance (FR K; OMB No. 7100-0310).
