# Semiannual Regulatory Flexibility Agenda

**Proposed Rule** | 79 FR 76850 | Published 2014-12-22

**Document Number:** 2014-28990

---

## Action

Semiannual regulatory agenda.

## Abstract

The Board is issuing this agenda under the Regulatory Flexibility Act and the Board's Statement of Policy Regarding Expanded Rulemaking Procedures. The Board anticipates having under consideration regulatory matters as indicated below during the period November 1, 2014, through April 30, 2015. The next agenda will be published in spring 2015.
