# Agency Information Collection Activities: Announcement of Board Approval Under Delegated Authority and Submission to OMB

**Notice** | 88 FR 88921 | Published 2023-12-26

**Document Number:** 2023-28345

---

## Action

On August 18, 2023, the Board published a notice in the Federal Register (88 FR 56628) requesting public comment for 60 days on the extension, without revision, of the FR H-6. The comment period for this notice expired on October 17, 2023. The Board did not receive any comments.

## Abstract

The Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System (Board) is adopting a proposal to extend for three years, without revision, the Reporting Requirements Associated with section 208.22 of Regulation H (Notifications Related to Community Development and Public Welfare Investments by State Member Banks) (FR H-6; OMB No. 7100-0278).
