# Reimbursement for Providing Financial Records; Recordkeeping Requirements for Certain Financial Records

**Proposed Rule** | 61 FR 43195 | Published 1996-08-21

**Document Number:** 96-21264

---

## Action

Proposed rule.

## Abstract

The Board is proposing an amendment to subpart B of its Regulation S. Subpart B cross-references the substantive provisions of a joint rule adopted by the Board and the Department of the Treasury relating to the recordkeeping requirements for funds transfers and transmittals of funds. The proposed amendment would clarify that Regulation S does not apply to any person or transaction or class of persons or transactions that the Treasury has exempted from the joint rule.
