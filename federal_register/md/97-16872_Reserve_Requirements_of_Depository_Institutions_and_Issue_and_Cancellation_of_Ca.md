# Reserve Requirements of Depository Institutions and Issue and Cancellation of Capital Stock of Federal Reserve Banks

**Final Rule** | 62 FR 34613 | Published 1997-06-27
**Effective:** 1997-10-01
**Document Number:** 97-16872

---

## Action

Final rule.

## Abstract

The Board is amending Regulations D and I, Reserve Requirements of Depository Institutions and Issue and Cancellation of Capital Stock of Federal Reserve Banks, respectively, to define the location of a depository institution for purposes of Federal Reserve membership and reserve account maintenance. These amendments will facilitate interstate banking.
