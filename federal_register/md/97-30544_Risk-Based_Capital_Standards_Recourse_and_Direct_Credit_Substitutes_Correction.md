# Risk-Based Capital Standards; Recourse and Direct Credit Substitutes; Correction

**Proposed Rule** | 62 FR 62234 | Published 1997-11-20

**Document Number:** 97-30544

---

## Action

Joint notice of proposed rulemaking; correction.

## Abstract

The Office of the Comptroller of the Currency, the Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System, the Federal Deposit Insurance Corporation, and the Office of Thrift Supervision (collectively, the agencies) are issuing a correction to its joint notice of proposed rulemaking published on November 5, 1997 to clarify three of the tables contained in the preamble to the rule. The proposed rule would revise the agencies' risk-based capital standards to address recourse and direct credit substitutes.
