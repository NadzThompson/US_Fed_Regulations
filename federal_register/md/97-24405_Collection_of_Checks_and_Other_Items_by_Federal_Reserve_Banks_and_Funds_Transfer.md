# Collection of Checks and Other Items by Federal Reserve Banks and Funds Transfers Through Fedwire

**Final Rule** | 62 FR 48166 | Published 1997-09-15

**Document Number:** 97-24405

---

## Action

Final rule.

## Abstract

Effective January 2, 1998, the Reserve Banks will begin to implement a policy under which each depository institution may maintain only a single funds account with the Federal Reserve. A single account will establish a single debtor-creditor relationship between each institution and a Federal Reserve Bank and will make account management more efficient for banks with interstate branches. The Board is adopting amendments to subpart A of Regulation J to conform the Federal Reserve check collection rules to the single account structure.
