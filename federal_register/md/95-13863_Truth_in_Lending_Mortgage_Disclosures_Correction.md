# Truth in Lending; Mortgage Disclosures; Correction

**Final Rule** | 60 FR 29969 | Published 1995-06-07
**Effective:** 1995-06-07
**Document Number:** 95-13863

---

## Action

Corrections to final regulation.

## Abstract

This document contains corrections to the final rule (Docket No. R-0858) which was published Friday, March 24, 1995 (60 FR 15463). The amendments to Regulation Z concerned new disclosure requirements on certain home loans bearing rates or fees above a certain percentage or amount and on reverse mortgage transactions.
