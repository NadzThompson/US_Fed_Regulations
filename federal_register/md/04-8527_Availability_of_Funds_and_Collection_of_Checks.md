# Availability of Funds and Collection of Checks

**Final Rule** | 69 FR 19921 | Published 2004-04-15
**Effective:** 2004-06-26
**Document Number:** 04-8527

---

## Action

Final rule; technical amendment.

## Abstract

The Board of Governors is amending appendix A of Regulation CC to delete the reference to the Charleston check processing office of the Federal Reserve Bank of Richmond and reassign the Federal Reserve routing symbols currently listed under that office to the Cincinnati office of the Federal Reserve Bank of Cleveland. These amendments reflect the restructuring of check processing operations within the Federal Reserve System.
