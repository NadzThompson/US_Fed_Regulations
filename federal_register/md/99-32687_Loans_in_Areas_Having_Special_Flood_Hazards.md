# Loans in Areas Having Special Flood Hazards

**Final Rule** | 64 FR 71272 | Published 1999-12-21
**Effective:** 1999-12-21
**Document Number:** 99-32687

---

## Action

Joint final rule.

## Abstract

The Agencies jointly are making technical amendments to their regulations on loans in areas having special flood hazards. This action removes an outdated cross-reference to Federal Emergency Management Agency (FEMA) regulations that had contained the text of the Standard Flood Hazard Determination Form (Form). This action is intended to update and make accurate the Agencies' regulations regarding loans in areas having special flood hazards.
