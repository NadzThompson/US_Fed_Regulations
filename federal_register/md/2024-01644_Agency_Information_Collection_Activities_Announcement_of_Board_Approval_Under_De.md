# Agency Information Collection Activities: Announcement of Board Approval Under Delegated Authority and Submission to OMB

**Notice** | 89 FR 5540 | Published 2024-01-29

**Document Number:** 2024-01644

---

## Action

On July 19, 2023, the Board published a notice in the Federal Register (88 FR 46164) requesting public comment for 60 days on the extension, without revision, of the FR H-5. The comment period for this notice expired on September 18, 2023. The Board did not receive any comments relevant to this collection or to the PRA.

## Abstract

The Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System (Board) is adopting a proposal to extend for three years, without revision, the Recordkeeping Requirements Associated with Regulation H (Real Estate Lending Standards Regulation for State Member Banks) (FR H-5; OMB No. 7100-0261).
