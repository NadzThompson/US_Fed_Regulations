# Proposed Agency Information Collection Activities; Comment Request

**Notice** | 89 FR 5540 | Published 2024-01-29

**Document Number:** 2024-01645

---

## Action

Notice, request for comment.

## Abstract

The Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System (Board) invites comment on a proposal to extend for three years, without revision, the Interagency Notice of Change in Control, Interagency Notice of Change in Director or Senior Executive Officer, and Interagency Biographical and Financial Report (FR 2081a, b, and c; OMB No. 7100-0134).
