# Equal Credit Opportunity

**Proposed Rule** | 61 FR 68688 | Published 1996-12-30

**Document Number:** 96-33088

---

## Action

Withdrawal of proposed rule.

## Abstract

The Board is withdrawing a proposed amendment to Regulation B (Equal Credit Opportunity) that would have eliminated the general prohibition on collecting data relating to an applicant's sex, race, color, religion, and national origin.
