# Loans to Executive Officers, Directors, and Principal Shareholders of Member Banks; Loans to Holding Companies and Affiliates

**Final Rule** | 60 FR 31053 | Published 1995-06-13
**Effective:** 1995-07-01
**Document Number:** 95-14413

---

## Action

Final rule.

## Abstract

The Board is adopting an amendment to Regulation O to conform the definition of unimpaired capital and unimpaired surplus used in calculating a bank's Regulation O lending limit to the definition of capital and surplus recently adopted by the Office of the Comptroller of the Currency in calculating the limit on loans by a national bank to a single borrower. The final rule will reduce the regulatory burden for member banks monitoring lending to their insiders.
