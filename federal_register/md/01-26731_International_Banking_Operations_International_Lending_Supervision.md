# International Banking Operations; International Lending Supervision

**Proposed Rule** | 66 FR 54399 | Published 2001-10-26

**Document Number:** 01-26731

---

## Action

Notice of proposed rulemaking.

## Abstract

The Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System (Board) is seeking public comment on a proposal to amend its regulations relating to international lending by simplifying the discussion concerning the accounting for fees on international loans to make the regulation consistent with generally accepted accounting principles (GAAP).
