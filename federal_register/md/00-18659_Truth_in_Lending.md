# Truth in Lending

**Proposed Rule** | 65 FR 45547 | Published 2000-07-24

**Document Number:** 00-18659

---

## Action

Public hearings; notice of additional site.

## Abstract

On July 12, 2000, the Board published a notice of three public hearings on predatory lending practices in the home-equity lending market, and invited consumers, consumer advocacy organizations, lenders, and other interested parties to attend and to provide written comments on relevant issues. The Board will hold a fourth hearing in Chicago, and is republishing the earlier notice with information about the additional site.
