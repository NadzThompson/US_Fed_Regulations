# Agency Information Collection Activities: Announcement of Board Approval Under Delegated Authority and Submission to OMB

**Notice** | 88 FR 66850 | Published 2023-09-28

**Document Number:** 2023-21157

---

## Action

On May 15, 2023, the Board published an initial notice in the Federal Register (88 FR 30972) requesting public comment for 60 days on the implementation of the FR 3100. The comment period for this notice expired on July 14, 2023. The Board did not receive any comments. The collection will be implemented as proposed.

## Abstract

The Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System (Board) is adopting a proposal to implement the Ad Hoc Clearance for Board-Wide Use (FR 3100; OMB No. 7100-NEW).
