# Equal Credit Opportunity

**Final Rule** | 73 FR 53685 | Published 2008-09-17
**Effective:** 2008-10-17
**Document Number:** E8-21629

---

## Action

Final rule; technical amendment.

## Abstract

The Board is publishing amendments to Regulation B (Equal Credit Opportunity Act) to update the address where questions should be directed concerning creditors for which the Office of Thrift Supervision administers compliance with the regulation.
