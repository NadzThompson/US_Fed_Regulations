# Reserve Requirements of Depository Institutions

**Final Rule** | 63 FR 64841 | Published 1998-11-24
**Effective:** 1998-11-24
**Document Number:** 98-31354

---

## Action

Final rule; technical amendment.

## Abstract

The Board is amending Regulation D (Reserve Requirements of Depository Institutions) to remove the definition of De novo depository institution. The definition is not used in the Regulation.
