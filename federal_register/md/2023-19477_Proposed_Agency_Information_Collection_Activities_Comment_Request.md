# Proposed Agency Information Collection Activities; Comment Request

**Notice** | 88 FR 62363 | Published 2023-09-11

**Document Number:** 2023-19477

---

## Action

Notice, request for comment.

## Abstract

The Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System (Board) invites comment on a proposal to extend for three years, with revision, the Recordkeeping and Disclosure Requirements Associated with Regulation R (FR R; OMB No. 7100-0316).
