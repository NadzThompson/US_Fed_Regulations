# Privacy Act of 1974; Implementation

**Final Rule** | 67 FR 44526 | Published 2002-07-03

**Document Number:** 02-16725

---

## Action

Final rule.

## Abstract

The Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System (Board) is amending its Rules Regarding Access to Personal Information under the Privacy Act (Privacy Act Rules) to reflect the implementation of a new system of records. Notice of this new system of records, entitled Visitor Log (BGFRS-32) is published in an adjacent notice.
