# Proposed Agency Information Collection Activities; Comment Request

**Notice** | 89 FR 12344 | Published 2024-02-16

**Document Number:** 2024-03218

---

## Action

Notice, request for comment.

## Abstract

The Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System (Board) invites comment on a proposal to extend for three years, without revision, the Recordkeeping Requirements Associated with Regulation F (FR F; OMB No. 7100-0331).
