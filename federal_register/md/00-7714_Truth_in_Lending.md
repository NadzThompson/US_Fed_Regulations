# Truth in Lending

**Final Rule** | 65 FR 17129 | Published 2000-03-31
**Effective:** 2000-03-24
**Document Number:** 00-7714

---

## Action

Final rule.

## Abstract

The Board is publishing revisions to the official staff commentary to Regulation Z (Truth in Lending). The commentary applies and interprets the requirements of Regulation Z. The revisions address short-term cash advances commonly called "payday loans." The Board is also publishing technical corrections to the commentary and regulation.
