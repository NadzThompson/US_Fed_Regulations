# Management Official Interlocks

**Final Rule** | 61 FR 40293 | Published 1996-08-02
**Effective:** 1996-10-01
**Document Number:** 96-19400

---

## Action

Joint final rule.

## Abstract

The Office of the Comptroller of the Currency (OCC), Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System (Board), Federal Deposit Insurance Corporation (FDIC), and Office of Thrift Supervision (OTS) (collectively, the agencies) are revising their rules regarding management interlocks. This final rule conforms the interlocks rules to recent statutory changes, modernizes and clarifies the rules, and reduces unnecessary regulatory burdens where feasible, consistent with statutory requirements. In so doing, it reflects comments received on the proposed rule and the agencies' further internal considerations.
