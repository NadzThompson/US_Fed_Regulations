# Bank Holding Companies and Change in Bank Control

**Final Rule** | 68 FR 68493 | Published 2003-12-09
**Effective:** 2004-01-08
**Document Number:** 03-29997

---

## Action

Final rule.

## Abstract

The Board is amending Regulation Y to expand the ability of all bank holding companies, including financial holding companies, to process, store and transmit nonfinancial data in connection with their financial data processing, storage and transmission activities. Specifically, the Board is raising the revenue limit that currently applies to the nonfinancial data processing activities of bank holding companies from 30 percent to 49 percent. The Board also announced that it will consider proposals by a financial holding company to engage in, or acquire a company engaged in, other nonfinancial data processing, information portal, and technology-related activities that the financial holding company believes are complementary to financial activities on a case-by-case basis in accordance with the procedures established by section 4(j) of the Bank Holding Company Act.
