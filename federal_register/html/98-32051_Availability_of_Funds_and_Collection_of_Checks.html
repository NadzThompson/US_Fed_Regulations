Availability of Funds and Collection of Checks

Type: Proposed Rule | Citation: 63 FR 66499

Published: 1998-12-02

Document Number: 98-32051

Action

Notice of proposed rulemaking.

Abstract

The Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System (the Board) is proposing to amend Regulation CC to allow banks that consummate a merger on or after July 1, 1998, and before June 1, 1999, greater time to implement software changes related to the merger.
