Rules Regarding Equal Opportunity

Type: Proposed Rule | Citation: 60 FR 45385

Published: 1995-08-31

Document Number: 95-21616

Action

Notice of proposed rulemaking.

Abstract

The Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System (the Board) is seeking public comment on a proposed amendment to its Rules Regarding Equal Opportunity which corrects an ambiguity in the provision regarding access to the investigative file. The Rules set out the complaint processing procedures governing complaints by Board employees and applicants for employment alleging discrimination in employment, and related matters.
