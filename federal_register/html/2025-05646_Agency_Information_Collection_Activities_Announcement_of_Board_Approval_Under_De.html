Agency Information Collection Activities: Announcement of Board Approval Under Delegated Authority and Submission to OMB

Type: Notice | Citation: 90 FR 14444

Published: 2025-04-02

Document Number: 2025-05646

Action

On October 29, 2024, the Board published a notice in the Federal Register (89 FR 85971) requesting public comment for 60 days on the extension, without revision, of the FR 4027. The comment period for this notice expired on December 30, 2024. The Board did not receive any comments.

Abstract

The Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System (Board) is adopting a proposal to extend for three years, without revision, the Recordkeeping Provisions Associated with the Guidance on Sound Incentive Compensation Policies (FR 4027; OMB No. 7100-0327).
