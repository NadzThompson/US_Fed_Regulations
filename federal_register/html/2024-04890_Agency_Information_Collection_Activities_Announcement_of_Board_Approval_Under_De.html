Agency Information Collection Activities: Announcement of Board Approval Under Delegated Authority and Submission to OMB

Type: Notice | Citation: 89 FR 16570

Published: 2024-03-07

Document Number: 2024-04890

Action

On August 18, 2023, the Board published an initial notice in the Federal Register (88 FR 56627) requesting public comment for 60 days on the extension, without revision, of the FR DD. The comment period for this notice expired on October 17, 2023. The Board did not receive any comments.

Abstract

The Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System (Board) is adopting a proposal to extend for three years, without revision, the Disclosure Requirements Associated with the Consumer Financial Protection Bureau's (CFPB) Regulation DD (FR DD; OMB No. 7100-0271).
