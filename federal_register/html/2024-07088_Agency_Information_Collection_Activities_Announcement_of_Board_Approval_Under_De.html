Agency Information Collection Activities: Announcement of Board Approval Under Delegated Authority and Submission to OMB

Type: Notice | Citation: 89 FR 23015

Published: 2024-04-03

Document Number: 2024-07088

Action

On November 15, 2023, the Board published a notice in the Federal Register (88 FR 78363) requesting public comment for 60 days on the extension, without revision, of the FR 28. The comment period for this notice expired on January 16, 2024. The Board did not receive any comments.

Abstract

The Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System (Board) is adopting a proposal to extend for three years, without revision, the Applications for Employment with the Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System (FR 28; OMB No. 7100-0181).
