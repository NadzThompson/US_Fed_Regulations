Agency Information Collection Activities: Announcement of Board Approval Under Delegated Authority and Submission to OMB

Type: Notice | Citation: 88 FR 62370

Published: 2023-09-11

Document Number: 2023-19473

Action

On May 15, 2023, the Board published a notice in the Federal Register (88 FR 30973) requesting public comment for 60 days on the extension, with revision, of the FR 29. The Board proposed to revise the FR 29 to no longer include the FR 29a, as it was determined not to be subject to the PRA. The comment period for this notice expired on July 14, 2023. The Board did not receive any comments. The revisions will be implemented as proposed.

Abstract

The Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System (Board) is adopting a proposal to extend for three years, with revision, the Compensation and Salary Surveys (FR 29; OMB No. 7100-0290).
