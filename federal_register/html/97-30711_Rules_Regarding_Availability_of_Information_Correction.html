Type: Final Rule | Citation: 62 FR 62508

Published: 1997-11-24 | Effective: 1997-11-19

Abstract

The Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System (Board) published in the Federal Register of October 20, 1997, a document which amended the Board's Rules Regarding Availability of Information (Rules). This document corrects citation errors within the Rules.
