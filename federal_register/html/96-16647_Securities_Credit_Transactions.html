Securities Credit Transactions

Type: Proposed Rule | Citation: 61 FR 33874

Published: 1996-07-01

Document Number: 96-16647

Action

Proposed rule; extension of comment period.

Abstract

The Board is extending the comment period on its proposal to amend its margin regulations, Regulations G, T, and U, to give the public additional time to comment on the proposal. The Secretary of the Board, acting pursuant to delegated authority, has extended the comment period from July 1, 1996 to August 2, 1996, to give the public additional time to provide comments.
