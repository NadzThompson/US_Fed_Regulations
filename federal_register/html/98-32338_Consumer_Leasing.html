Consumer Leasing

Type: Proposed Rule | Citation: 63 FR 67434

Published: 1998-12-07

Document Number: 98-32338

Action

Proposed rule; official staff commentary.

Abstract

The Board is publishing for comment proposed revisions to the official staff commentary to Regulation M, which implements the Consumer Leasing Act. The commentary applies and interprets the requirements of the regulation. The proposed update would provide guidance on disclosures for lease advertisements, multiple-item leases, renegotiations and extensions and estimates of official fees and taxes.
