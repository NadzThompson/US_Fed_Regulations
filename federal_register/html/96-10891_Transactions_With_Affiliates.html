Transactions With Affiliates

Type: Final Rule | Citation: 61 FR 19805

Published: 1996-05-03 | Effective: 1996-07-01

Document Number: 96-10891

Action

Final rule.

Abstract

The Board is adopting a definition of capital stock and surplus for purposes of section 23A of the Federal Reserve Act that conforms to the definition of unimpaired capital and unimpaired surplus used by the Board in calculating the limits in Regulation O for insider lending and by the Office of the Comptroller of the Currency (OCC) in calculating the limit on loans by a national bank to a single borrower. The final rule will reduce the burden for member banks and other insured depository institutions monitoring lending to their affiliates.
