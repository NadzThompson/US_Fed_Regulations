Collection of Checks and Other Items by Federal Reserve Banks and Funds Transfers Through Fedwire

Type: Proposed Rule | Citation: 69 FR 34086

Published: 2004-06-18

Document Number: 04-13147

Action

Proposed rule.

Abstract

The Board of Governors is publishing for comment a proposed rule that would amend subpart A of Regulation J to provide for the rights and obligations of sending banks, paying banks, returning banks, and Reserve Banks in connection with collection of substitute checks and items that have been converted to electronic form. The proposed changes would ensure that Regulation J covers the new check processing service options that the Reserve Banks plan to offer when the Check Clearing for the 21st Century Act becomes effective on October 28, 2004.
