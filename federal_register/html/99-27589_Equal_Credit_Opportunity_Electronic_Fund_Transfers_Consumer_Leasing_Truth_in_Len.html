Equal Credit Opportunity; Electronic Fund Transfers; Consumer Leasing; Truth in Lending; Truth in Savings

Type: Proposed Rule | Citation: 64 FR 57409

Published: 1999-10-25

Document Number: 99-27589

Action

Request for comments; extension of comment period.

Abstract

On September 14, 1999, the Board published revised proposals for public comment that would permit electronic delivery of federally mandated disclosures under five consumer protection regulations: B (Equal Credit Opportunity), E (Electronic Fund Transfers), M (Consumer Leasing), Z (Truth in Lending), and DD (Truth in Savings). The Board is extending the comment period to give the public additional time to provide comments.
