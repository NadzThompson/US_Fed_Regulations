Loans to Executive Officers, Directors, and Principal Shareholders of Member Banks

Type: Final Rule | Citation: 86 FR 27507

Published: 2021-05-21 | Effective: 2021-05-21

Document Number: 2021-10711

Action

Interim final rule with request for comment.

Abstract

On April 17, 2020, the Board issued an interim final rule to except certain loans made through June 30, 2020, that are guaranteed under the Small Business Administration's Paycheck Protection Program from the requirements of the Federal Reserve Act and the associated provisions of the Board's Regulation O. The Board issued two additional interim final rules to extend the exception when Congress approved extensions to the Paycheck Protection Program. To reflect a further extension approved by Congress and to automatically capture any further extensions, the Board is issuing this interim final rule to extend this exception to such loans made through March 31, 2022.
