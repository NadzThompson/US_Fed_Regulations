Agency Information Collection Activities: Announcement of Board Approval Under Delegated Authority and Submission to OMB

Type: Notice | Citation: 88 FR 19143

Published: 2023-03-30

Document Number: 2023-06553

Action

On November 4, 2022, the Board published a notice in the Federal Register (87 FR 66699) requesting public comment for 60 days on the extension, with revision, of the FR LL-10(b). The Board proposed to revise the FR LL-10(b) by clearing the deregistration process described in Supervision and Regulation Letter 11-12; adding additional language clarifying that a respondent must retain a copy of the signed form at least three years, unless it ceases to be an SLHC before then; adding additional language on the Board's procedures for requesting confidential treatment, including the relevant regulatory citation; clarifying that the background information requested includes formations of savings associations or SLHCs; clarifying that the requested information includes formations of SLHCs and related subsidiaries; and clarifying that the documentation requirements include formations of savings associations and SLHCs, and that responses should be limited to changes since submitting the SLHC application. The Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System (Board) is adopting a proposal to extend for three years, with revision, the Savings and Loan Holding Company Registration Statement (FR LL-10(b); OMB No. 7100-0337).
