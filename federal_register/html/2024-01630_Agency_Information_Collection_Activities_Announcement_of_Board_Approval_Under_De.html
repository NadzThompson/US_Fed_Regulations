Agency Information Collection Activities: Announcement of Board Approval Under Delegated Authority and Submission to OMB

Type: Notice | Citation: 89 FR 5543

Published: 2024-01-29

Document Number: 2024-01630

Action

On September 11, 2023, the Board published a notice in the Federal Register (88 FR 62366) requesting public comment for 60 days on the extension, without revision, of the FR H-3. The comment period for this notice expired on November 13, 2023.

Abstract

The Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System (Board) is adopting a proposal to extend for three years, without revision, the Recordkeeping and Disclosure Requirements Associated with Regulation H (Securities Transactions by State Member Banks) (FR H-3; OMB No. 7100- 0196).
