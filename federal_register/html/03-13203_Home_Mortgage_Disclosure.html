Home Mortgage Disclosure

Type: Final Rule | Citation: 68 FR 31589

Published: 2003-05-28 | Effective: 2003-06-27

Document Number: 03-13203

Action

Final rules; official staff commentary.

Abstract

The Board is publishing final amendments to the official staff commentary to Regulation C (Home Mortgage Disclosure). The amendments provide transition rules for applications received before January 1, 2004, on which final action is taken on or after January 1, 2004.
