Home Mortgage Disclosure

Type: Final Rule | Citation: 67 FR 43217

Published: 2002-06-27 | Effective: 2003-01-01

Document Number: 02-16189

Action

Final rule; technical amendment.

Abstract

The Board is publishing amendments to Regulation C (Home Mortgage Disclosure). The amendments require lenders to ask applicants their race or national origin and sex in applications taken by telephone, conforming the telephone application rule to the rule applicable to mail and Internet applications.
