Rules Regarding Availability of Information

Type: Proposed Rule | Citation: 84 FR 27976

Published: 2019-06-17

Document Number: 2019-12524

Action

Notice of proposed rulemaking and request for comment.

Abstract

The Board is inviting comment on a notice of proposed rulemaking (proposal) that would amend the Board's Rules Regarding Availability of Information (Board's Rules). The amendments clarify and update the Board's regulations implementing the Freedom of Information Act and the rules governing the disclosure of confidential supervisory information and other nonpublic information of the Board.
