Credit by Brokers and Dealers; Security Futures

Type: Proposed Rule | Citation: 67 FR 62214

Published: 2002-10-04

Document Number: 02-25227

Action

Proposed rule.

Abstract

The Board of Governors is publishing for comment proposed amendments to Regulation T to clarify the treatment of stock futures held by customers at a security futures intermediary. The proposed amendments would permit the withdrawal of cash from an otherwise restricted margin account if required to satisfy maintenance margin requirements. The proposed amendments also would explicitly permit stock futures to be carried in a good faith account as is currently done with other futures.
