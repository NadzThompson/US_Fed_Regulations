Bank Holding Companies and Change in Bank Control: Technical Amendments

Type: Final Rule | Citation: 66 FR 19081

Published: 2001-04-13 | Effective: 2001-04-13

Document Number: 01-9123

Action

Final rule and interim rule; technical amendments.

Abstract

The Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System (Board) is adopting technical amendments to the financial holding company provisions of Regulation Y to restore provisions that were adopted in December 2000, and inadvertently deleted from the Code of Federal Regulations.
