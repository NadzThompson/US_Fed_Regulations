Truth in Lending

Type: Proposed Rule | Citation: 62 FR 5183

Published: 1997-02-04

Document Number: 97-2293

Action

Proposed rule.

Abstract

The Board is publishing for comment proposed revisions to Regulation Z. The revisions implement an amendment to the Truth in Lending Act contained in the Economic Growth and Regulatory Paperwork Reduction Act of 1996 affecting the disclosure of a fifteen-year historical example of rates and payments. The amendment applies to variable-rate loans with a term exceeding one year and secured by the consumer's principal dwelling. The amendment allows creditors either to disclose a fifteen-year historical example or to give a statement that the periodic payment may substantially increase or decrease together with a maximum interest rate and payment based on a $10,000 loan.
