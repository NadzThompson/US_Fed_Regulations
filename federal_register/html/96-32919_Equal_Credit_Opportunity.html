Equal Credit Opportunity

Type: Proposed Rule | Citation: 62 FR 56

Published: 1997-01-02

Document Number: 96-32919

Action

Proposed rule.

Abstract

The Board is publishing for comment proposed revisions to Regulation B (Equal Credit Opportunity). The revisions would implement recent amendments to the Equal Credit Opportunity Act (ECOA). These amendments create a legal privilege for information developed by creditors as a result of ``self-tests'' that they voluntarily conduct to determine the level of their compliance with the ECOA. The Department of Housing and Urban Development will be publishing for comment a substantially similar proposal to revise the regulations implementing the Fair Housing Act.
