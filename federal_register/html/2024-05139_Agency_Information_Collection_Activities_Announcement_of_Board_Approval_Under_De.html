Agency Information Collection Activities: Announcement of Board Approval Under Delegated Authority and Submission to OMB

Type: Notice | Citation: 89 FR 17849

Published: 2024-03-12 | Effective: 2024-04-11

Document Number: 2024-05139

Action

On September 8, 2023, the Board published a notice in the Federal Register (88 FR 62084) requesting public comment for 60 days on the implementation of the FR 30. The comment period for this notice expired on November 7, 2023. The Board did not receive any comments. The FR 30 will be implemented as originally proposed.

Abstract

The Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System (Board) is adopting a proposal to implement the Whistleblower Intake Guide (FR 30; OMB No. 7100-NEW).
