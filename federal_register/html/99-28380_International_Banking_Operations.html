Type: Final Rule | Citation: 64 FR 58780

Published: 1999-11-01 | Effective: 1999-11-01

Document Number: 99-28380

Action

Interpretation.

Abstract

The Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System has issued an interpretation concerning the scope of the data processing provision of Regulation K. The interpretation clarifies that a banking organization may not engage in a broader range of data processing activities outside the United States under Regulation K than is permissible under Regulation Y, without the Board's approval.
