Securities Credit Transactions; List of Marginable OTC Stocks; List of Foreign Margin Stocks; Regulations G, T, U and X

Type: Final Rule | Citation: 60 FR 5845

Published: 1995-01-31 | Effective: 1995-02-13

Document Number: 95-2301

Action

Final rule; determination of applicability of regulations.

Abstract

The List of Marginable OTC Stocks (OTC List) is composed of stocks traded over-the-counter (OTC) in the United States that have been determined by the Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System to be subject to the margin requirements under certain Federal Reserve regulations. The List of Foreign Margin Stocks (Foreign List) is composed of foreign equity securities that have met the Board's eligibility criteria under Regulation T. The OTC List and the Foreign List are published four times a year by the Board. This document sets forth additions to and deletions from the previous OTC List and deletions from the previous Foreign List.
