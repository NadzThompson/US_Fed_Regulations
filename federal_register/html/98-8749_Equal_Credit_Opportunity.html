Equal Credit Opportunity

Type: Final Rule | Citation: 63 FR 16392

Published: 1998-04-03 | Effective: 1998-04-30

Document Number: 98-8749

Action

Final rule.

Abstract

The Board is amending certain model forms in its Regulation B to reflect statutory amendments to the Fair Credit Reporting Act (FCRA) disclosures contained in those forms. Creditors have the option of including the FCRA disclosures with the notice of action taken required under Regulation B. In addition, a technical revision has been made to Appendix A.
