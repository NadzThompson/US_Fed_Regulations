Management Official Interlocks

Type: Proposed Rule | Citation: 60 FR 67424

Published: 1995-12-29

Document Number: 95-30972

Action

Joint notice of proposed rulemaking.

Abstract

The Office of the Comptroller of the Currency (OCC), Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System (Board), Federal Deposit Insurance Corporation (FDIC), and Office of Thrift Supervision (OTS) (collectively, the agencies) propose to revise their rules regarding management interlocks. The proposal conforms the interlocks rules to recent statutory changes, modernizes and clarifies the rules, and reduces unnecessary regulatory burdens where feasible, consistent with statutory requirements.
