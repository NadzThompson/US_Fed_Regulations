Home Mortgage Disclosure

Type: Proposed Rule | Citation: 60 FR 30013

Published: 1995-06-07

Document Number: 95-13861

Action

Proposed rule; staff interpretation.

Abstract

The Board is publishing for comment a staff commentary to Regulation C (Home Mortgage Disclosure). The commentary applies and interprets the requirements of Regulation C. The proposed commentary provides guidance on various issues including the treatment under Regulation C of prequalifications, participations, refinancings, home [[Page 30014]] equity lines, mergers, and loan applications received through a broker. The Board believes the proposed commentary will reduce burden and ease compliance by clarifying a number of issues, by providing flexibility in compliance, and by consolidating the guidance that is currently available from a variety of sources.
