Equal Credit Opportunity

Type: Proposed Rule | Citation: 63 FR 14552

Published: 1998-03-25

Document Number: 98-6992

Action

Proposed rule.

Abstract

The Board is publishing for comment a proposed rule amending Regulation B, which implements the Equal Credit Opportunity Act. The proposal would permit creditors to use electronic communication (for example, communication via personal computer and modem) to provide disclosures required by the act and regulation if the consumer agrees to such delivery.
