Bank Holding Companies and Change in Bank Control

Type: Final Rule | Citation: 68 FR 39807

Published: 2003-07-03 | Effective: 2003-08-04

Document Number: 03-16835

Action

Final rule.

Abstract

The Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System is adopting an amendment to Regulation Y that would permit bank holding companies to (i) take and make delivery of title to commodities underlying commodity derivative contracts on an instantaneous, pass-through basis; and (ii) enter into certain commodity derivative contracts that do not require cash settlement or specifically provide for assignment, termination, or offset prior to delivery.
