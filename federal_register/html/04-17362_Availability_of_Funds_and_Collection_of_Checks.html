Availability of Funds and Collection of Checks

Type: Final Rule | Citation: 69 FR 47290

Published: 2004-08-04 | Effective: 2004-10-28

Document Number: 04-17362

Action

Final rule.

Abstract

The Board of Governors is publishing final amendments to Regulation CC that add a new subpart D, with commentary, to implement the Check Clearing for the 21st Century Act. These amendments set forth the requirements of the Act that apply to banks, a model consumer awareness disclosure and other model notices, and indorsement and identification requirements for substitute checks. The final amendments also clarify some existing provisions of the rule and commentary.
