Agency Information Collection Activities: Announcement of Board Approval Under Delegated Authority and Submission to OMB

Type: Notice | Citation: 91 FR 1185

Published: 2026-01-12 | Effective: 2026-02-11

Document Number: 2026-00389

Action

On December 6, 2024, the Board published a notice in the Federal Register (89 FR 96977) requesting public comment for 60 days on the implementation of the FR 1413. The comment period for this notice expired on February 4, 2025. The Board did not receive any comments relevant to this collection or to the PRA. However, the Board has made additional de minimis changes to the forms to reflect updates to the Board's EEO policy since the end of the public comment period.

Abstract

The Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System (Board) is adopting a proposal to implement the Discrimination Complaint Forms (FR 1413; OMB No. 7100-NEW).
