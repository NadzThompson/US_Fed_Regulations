Reserve Requirements of Depository Institutions

Type: Proposed Rule | Citation: 62 FR 42708

Published: 1997-08-08

Document Number: 97-20957

Action

Proposed rule.

Abstract

The Board is proposing to amend its Regulation D, Reserve Requirements of Depository Institutions, to allow U.S. branches and agencies of foreign banks and Edge and Agreement corporations to choose whether to aggregate reserves on a nationwide basis in a single account at one Reserve Bank or to continue to have separate accounts on a same- state/same-District basis as they do today. The amendments would also update and clarify the pass-through account rules in Regulation D for all institutions. These amendments would facilitate interstate banking and eliminate certain restrictions applicable to pass-through accounts.
