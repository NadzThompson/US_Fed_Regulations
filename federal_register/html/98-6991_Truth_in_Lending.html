Truth in Lending

Type: Proposed Rule | Citation: 63 FR 14548

Published: 1998-03-25

Document Number: 98-6991

Action

Proposed rule.

Abstract

The Board is publishing for comment a proposed rule amending Regulation Z, which implements the Truth in Lending Act. The proposal would permit creditors to use electronic communication (for example, communication via personal computer and modem) to provide disclosures required by the act and regulation, if the consumer agrees to such delivery.
